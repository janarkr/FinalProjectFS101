R Subramaniam,60, SBS bus captain

Seeing his colleagues from Malaysia remaining in Singapore since the circuit breaker period, Bus Captain R Subramaniam decided to something to keep their spirits buoyant.

Since cooking was an enjoyable family activity to Subra, he decided along with his wife and daughter, to prepare home cooked meals for Malaysian drivers, especially Malaysian Indians who do not find Indian food easily available

"We see that eating home-cooked food makes these men calmer and more relaxed," said Mr Subra's daughter Vithiya, 32. "Food that is cooked as if for a family member or loved one, has a certain balance to it in its elements that make it appealing", the former chef said.
