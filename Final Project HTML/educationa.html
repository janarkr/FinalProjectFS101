Karthikeyan G, 35, English Teacher at Saint Joseph's Institution

Prior to the pandemic, English Literature teacher Mr Karthik brought his students on frequent tours around places which correlated with books.

Now, he mainly conducts his lessons online. Yet, he still meets a small sections of students mainly from the graduating cohorts.

"Many people talk about 2020 being cancelled. But where the education of our young students are concerns, 2020 or even 2021 should not be 'cancelled'. Our education system should run seamlessly and we teachers are here precisely for that," he said."
