Shaza Begum, 31, special needs educator, Northlight

Ms Shaza, teacher of Northlight School, has had her workload increased exponentially during the current pandemic, especially the circuit breaker. Children who were already identified under an "at risk" category prior to the pandemic, were in a even graver danger of slipping through the tracks

"I had to be extra vigilant, be it during an online classroom or an in person classroom setting," said the teacher of 10 year's experience. "Often they are from low-income families whose home situation are made more volatile by the pandemic"
